Your Home's Hidden Problem

Old Ducts Don't Just Lose Air — They Make Your Family Sick

Most Forney homeowners never see their ductwork. It runs through attic spaces and wall cavities, out of sight — but not out of effect. Ducts that are 10, 15, or 20 years old develop cracks, gaps, and collapsed sections. Through those openings, your system pulls air from wherever it can: attic insulation, crawl spaces, and wall cavities where moisture builds up and mold grows undetected.

That air mixes directly with what your family breathes every day. The result is more than discomfort. Chronic allergy flare-ups, worsening asthma, unexplained respiratory symptoms, and fatigue that no doctor traces back to a duct system nobody thought to inspect. Children and elderly residents feel it first — and hardest.

Ductwork replacement changes the equation. New ducts seal the system, restore proper airflow to every room, and remove the source — not just the symptom. Patching old ductwork or running a new AC unit through a failing duct system is money that never reaches you as conditioned air. Up to 30% of cooled or heated air escapes through cracks before it ever gets to a vent.

At Heshma Air Conditioning, we inspect, assess, and replace residential ductwork across Forney, Dallas, Mesquite, and Heath. Owner-operated, TACLA licensed, and priced straight — we tell you what it costs before we start, and charge exactly that.
